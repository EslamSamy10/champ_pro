About

Our vision

Re-formulation of the martial arts sport and its dissemination at the local level and for all targeted segments of society and raising it to the highest international levels to keep pace with the development in sports and reach the levels of continental and global competition in line with the Kingdom’s position and capabilities while building sustainable bases for the development of the game in the future

Our message

Preparing and equipping local athletes capable of continental and global competition in the presence of support, capabilities, facilities and availability of training competencies and qualified administrative cadres
